# **App Name**: ChainTrack Verify

## Core Features:

- Product Origin Registration: Manufacturers can register new products with essential details like ID, name, batch number, and manufacturing date, automatically generating a unique QR code for each product. This establishes the product's origin in the supply chain.
- Immutable Record Keeping: All supply chain events (manufacture, distribution, retail) are securely logged as unchangeable, hashed records in Firebase, simulating a blockchain structure to ensure data integrity and transparency.
- Distribution Event Recording: Distributors can record their involvement with a product by adding their name, location, and a timestamp, appending a new block to the product's immutable supply chain history.
- Retail Handover Logging: Retailers can log the final transfer of a product into the retail environment, including their name, location, and a timestamp, further extending the product's verifiable journey.
- Customer Product Verification: Customers can scan a product's QR code or manually enter its ID to instantly access and view its complete, verified, and tamper-proof supply chain history.
- Interactive Supply Chain Timeline: The product's journey is presented visually on the verification page as a clear, chronological timeline, detailing every step from manufacturing to its current status.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: #398FAC. This professional blue-green hue evokes trustworthiness and advanced technology, forming a reliable core for the interface.
- Background color: #F0F3F4. A very light, near-white shade with a hint of the primary hue, providing a clean and spacious canvas for content.
- Accent color: #52E0BD. A vibrant, complementary green provides clear contrast for interactive elements and highlights, maintaining visual harmony.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif font known for its clarity and readability, making it ideal for displaying critical supply chain data and information hierarchy.
- Utilize modern, clean line icons that convey security, verification, and transactional flow. Icons should be easily understandable and contribute to a sense of order and reliability.
- The layout emphasizes clarity and ease of navigation. Core modules are distinct, with forms designed for straightforward data entry. The product verification page features a clear, well-structured timeline display for historical data.
- Subtle and fluid animations, such as smooth transitions for content loading and state changes, will enhance the user experience without being distracting, reinforcing responsiveness and data flow.
